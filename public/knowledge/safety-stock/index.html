Safety stock

Inventory held to absorb uncertainty in demand or replenishment while protecting a chosen service objective.

InventoryDefinition

Definition

Safety stock is inventory deliberately held to absorb uncertainty during the replenishment cycle.

Example

If demand and lead time are variable, safety stock creates a buffer between expected consumption and the service commitment.
