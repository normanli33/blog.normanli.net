ABC–XYZ analysis

A portfolio segmentation that combines commercial importance with demand predictability.

SegmentationDefinition

Definition

ABC–XYZ analysis pairs an importance dimension with a variability dimension to avoid applying one policy to every item.

Application

Use segments to guide attention and policy—not as a substitute for judgement.
