## Trust is a product requirement
Fast answers are not useful if the user cannot inspect the evidence behind them.
## A safer interaction model
Every answer should expose its source, metric definition, filters, calculation path, and confidence limits.
## Key takeaways
Make verification easier than blind acceptance.
