## The problem

Vendor performance reviews ran on whichever spreadsheet the category manager happened to maintain — inconsistent metrics, no history, no way to compare vendors across categories.

## Approach

A single Power BI model combining on-time delivery, quality rejection rate, and price variance against contract, refreshed weekly from the ERP and quality systems.

## Result

Used for two quarterly vendor reviews before the procurement team moved to a vendor-management platform with built-in scorecarding — retired in favor of that, but it proved the metrics worth carrying forward into the new tool.
